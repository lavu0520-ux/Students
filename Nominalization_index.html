Technical English: Nominalization

Level: B2 Upper-Intermediate | Focus: Engineering & Academic Writing

What is Nominalization?

Nominalization is the process of turning verbs or adjectives into nouns. In engineering and academic writing, we use nominalization to make our language more formal, objective, and concise. It allows us to focus on concepts and processes rather than people or specific actions.

From Verb:
"We investigated the failure." → "The investigation was thorough."
From Adjective:
"The beam is strong." → "The strength of the beam is sufficient."
